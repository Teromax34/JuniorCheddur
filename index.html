What is Funky Station?
We're a fork (our GitHub) of a fork (Goob Station) of this game (Space Station 14).
We do some things differently, and have our own changes in the works. Check out our Discord for more.
Do I have to pay to play?
No! Space Station 14 is free for anyone to play, visit the official website linked here to see how to get the game on Steam or through its standalone launcher.
I love Funky Station! How can I help out?
Join our Discord to see how! If you'd like to support the community financially, please wait as official information is released about that!
Who's answering all these questions?!
The maintainer and host of Funky Station, taydeo! Speak to them directly on the official Funky Station Discord!
